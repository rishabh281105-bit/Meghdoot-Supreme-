Our Story

Five decades. One promise.

Bhagwan Electric Company has served Lucknow since the 1970s with a simple belief: stock quality electrical goods, give honest advice, and treat every customer with care.

In the 1970s, Laxman Prasad opened a small electrical shop in Lucknow's old city. The goal was clear from day one: dependable products, fair prices, and service people could trust.

That approach still drives the store today. Whether you need wires, fans, mixer grinders, irons, sockets, or daily electrical essentials, the focus remains the same: practical recommendations and reliable brands.
